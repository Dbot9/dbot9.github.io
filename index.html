Water Treatment

Indra’s patent pending water purification and treatment methodology is capable of a variety of water treatment needs through novel innovations in electrocoagulation, electrochemical oxidation, two-phase solids separation, disinfection, distillation and pollutant monitoring hardware. Our unique design and implementation of new technologies is more effective, affordable, and requires lower maintenance than most water treatment systems in the world. Our treatment solutions are modular and scalable depending on the throughput requirements. Applications range from greywater and black wastewater recycling, industrial wastewater management to drinking water purification

INDRA FLOW SERIES

INDRA FLOW SERIES is a pioneering Electro-Coagulation (EC) system designed to remove pollutants and pathogens in greywater and black water from residential, commercial, municipal or industrial buildings. Flow Series provides a low energy, chemical free alternative to chemical coagulation and other membrane or biological enzyme based processes.
Electrons and ions from sacrificial electrodes destabilize pollutants and encapsulate them within the large metal hydroxide flocs which can be separated out with our novel flow filtration technology. Flow EC combines with our novel ‘Activated Treatment’ process to provide continuous flow waste water treatment which is extremely potent, efficient and fast. Flow Series offers major environmental and financial benefits in the form of small footprint, lower capex and opex and chemical free water treatment. The versatility of the systems helps treat a wide variety of wastewater including those with high levels of suspended solids, dissolved heavy metals, emulsified oils, fats and greases, organic carbon, nutrients (nitrogen and phosphorous), viral and bacterial pathogens.

INDRA ACTIVATED FLOW SERIES

Industrial waste water with very high content of fine suspended solids, turbidity and coloration requires special treatment. Standard methodologies and filtration techniques are generally not very successful in separating these fine particulate pollutants from the waste water stream. INDRA ACTIVATED FLOW SERIES is a state of the art treatment system which combines our pioneering ‘Sludge Activated Flocculation’ (SAF) technology and ‘Activated Treatment’ process with the Flow EC system to remove such difficult pollutants, harmful chemicals and pathogens from industrial waste water streams. This continuous flow waste water treatment is extremely effective.

INDRA ELECTROX SERIES

The ‘ELECTROX SERIES’ is our most advanced industrial waste water treatment system. It successfully combines our pioneering Electro-chemical oxidation technology with Flow EC, SAF and ‘Activated Treatment’ processes to achieve multi-stage controlled waste water treatment in a continuous flow process. The Electrox Series is extremely effective in treating waste water with very high content of organic carbon and oxygen demanding substances. It also effectively deals with a host of other pollutants present in the industrial waste water effluent stream.

OXIDATION AND DISINFECTION

INDRA supplies state of the art Advanced Oxidation Process (AOP) solutions for the removal or neutralization of organic and inorganic contaminants from industrial wastewater. Our systems are very effective in reducing TOC, BOD and COD levels in wastewater including the removal of specific chemical species. We also provide advanced UV systems which incorporate our novel ‘Activated Treatment’ process for high levels of disinfection

SENSORS

The chemical, physical, and biological conditions of water form its quality. Even minute changes in these characteristics can impact the people and industries that depend on water. To preserve its quality, monitoring water parameters such as conductivity, pH, salinity, temperature, dissolved oxygen, chlorine residual and turbidity is crucial. For the same reason, water quality sensors have become common in most modern distribution systems.

MEDIA FILTERS

The INDRA filtration technology uses three different layers of mineral filter. Each layer has different particle sizes and different specific weights , arranged with decreasing grain size from top to bottom. This allows for the entire filter media to be used for filtration. This multi-layer filtration technology provides greater levels of filtration , longer filtration cycles and lower backwash water consumption compared to conventional sand filters.
